# Content Architect Skill (GEO-SEO & Citability)

Improve content for search and AI answer engines only from verified Kozbeyli
sources. Do not invent dates, scores, awards, guest reviews, prices,
availability, room facts, restoration details or local-history claims.

## Media And Provenance Rules

- Use approved hotel photos/videos first.
- Do not add Stable Diffusion, Midjourney or generated-image prompts to public
  product content.
- If a page needs a missing image, mark it as `media_needed` and list the real
  hotel content category required.
- Every factual addition must cite a repo file, evidence file, approved business
  document or live source-system reference.

## Component Suggestions

- Prefer existing components and route patterns in this repository.
- Suggest external UI references only as inspiration; do not auto-install or
  auto-publish third-party blocks.
- Keep public pages light, evidence-based and consistent with the stone/olive
  visual system.

## Core Content Checks

- Claim truthfulness: no precise age, distance, score or award without evidence.
- Reservation truthfulness: no guaranteed availability, discount or payment
  completion claim without HMS/POS evidence.
- Bilingual parity: Turkish primary, English concise and aligned.
- Self-containment: each page section should be understandable without reading
  another page.

## Brand Voice

- Sophisticated, welcoming, authoritative on local culture when sourced.
- Prefer "authentic", "curation", "historic texture" and "Aegean hospitality".
- Avoid "cheap", "budget", "basic" and motel-like positioning.

## Advanced Research & Competitive Benchmarking

Before adding content, identify the keyword/search intent, then separate:
verified facts, useful but unverified hypotheses, and content gaps that require
operator evidence.

## Generation Logic (Deep Strategy)

1. Audit existing page copy and evidence.
2. Draft only from verified inputs.
3. Mark unsupported additions as questions for the operator.
4. Finish with the source references, residual risks and validation command.
