# Voice and Tone Guidelines

## Voice
- **Authoritative**: We know the history of Kozbeyli better than anyone.
- **Warmly Sophisticated**: Like a well-read host welcoming a guest into their home.

## Tone by Platform
- **Website**: Elegant, descriptive, focus on sensory details.
- **Email**: Personal, helpful, concise.
- **Ads/Meta**: Punchy, result-oriented, urgency without "salesyness".

## Language Style
- Use proper Turkish (i.e., avoiding slang). 
- Use culinary and architectural terms accurately (e.g., "Kagir yapı", "Taş işçiliği", "Serpme kahvaltı").
